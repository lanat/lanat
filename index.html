About Letters From Lana

Letters from Lana originated from the simple desire to show people they are loved through a hand-crafted card.

Each card is inspired by a friend or family member. A portion of proceeds from each card will be donated to an organization related to the card’s materials or chosen by the card’s muse.

Follow me as I design one card a day for a year!
View designs on my blog (opens new page).

BRING BACK THE HANDWRITTEN CARD!

The thrill of receiving a piece of snail-mail, or a handwritten card means so much these days. I seek to share this love through my hand-crafted cards.

Card Muses

Each card is inspired by a friend or family member. Proceeds will be donated to an organization related to this card’s materials or chosen by the card’s muse.

Anna
  • Loves bows and whimsy
  • Last name match made in heaven
  • Pencils of promise

Anna

Bows, bikes, and whimsy – my good friend Anna is a ball of sunshine. She and I actually meant on account of our last names both starting with "TA" - one of the best roommate combos known to man. Anna and I used to talk about starting a non-profit where we donated schools supplies to children. We found Pencils of Promise which is exactly the model we wanted to take when we started our organization.

Charity: Pencils of Promise Website: pencilsofpromise.org/

Abbie
  • Movie star sister
  • Advised foxes are the rage
  • My planner buddy

Abbie

My sister Abbie (aka my "movie star sister") has been my role model all my life. When I need sound advice, I go to her. Even for the fun stuff, she tells me what's all the rage (hence the foxes) “It’s the most wonderful time of the year…” is the quote that can sum up my sister and my love for all things stationery. This was the Staples commercial branding strategy during the back-to-school season; which was literally, Abbie and my favorite time of year. Not only then, but October-November time when the new calendar year planners come out and we can start scouting out new planners to purchase. My love of cards and anything of the like can mostly be attributed to her and our bond over these items. She gave me some amazing stamps and ideas for my ‘forest animal’ line.

Charity: Boat People SOS Website: bpsos.org
Abby
  • Craft army captain
  • Number one card customer
  • Kid birthday parties

Abby

Abby and I got to experience Tokyo's finest craft stores over a weekend, where she was nicknamed our 'craft army captain.' She also happens to be one of my biggest card customers.One of my earliest memories of my friendship with Abby is our shared love of Paper Source. The rest is history as we share stationery finds around Shanghai, go to the Shanghai Kids Market for stickers, or just daydream about tassels and ribbon.

Charity: Included Website: included.org
Ba
  • Maps extraordinaire
  • Where we get our care gene from
  • Chain Bridge fishing trip

Ba

MAPS! Among the many traits I get from my father – my love of maps and stellar sense of direction stem from him. Now I understand why he spent hours studying maps before roadtrips, a characteristic only map lovers would understand. This line is dedicated to him. He also happened to be one of the most caring people...except when it comes to fish. He's an avid fisherman during the spring, which I have fond memories of as a little girl.

Charity: Boat People SOS Website: bpsos.org

My husband’s favorite animal is the frog, so it’s not even a question of what his card line would be. When asked if there was ‘go-to’ frog he would draw as a kid, this is what it looked like. For my forever prince charming, let’s save the frogs.

Charity: Save the Frogs Website: savethefrogs.com
